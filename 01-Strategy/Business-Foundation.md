# GlenRay Systems — Business Foundation

## Mission

Help traditional businesses implement practical, repeatable AI solutions that save time, reduce costs, and increase revenue — without the complexity.

## Vision

Become the go-to productized AI automation agency for small-to-mid-sized businesses that know they need AI but don't know where to start.

## Core Principles

1. **Simplicity over sophistication** — If the client can't explain it back to us, we over-engineered it.
2. **Repeatable over custom** — Every engagement should make the next one faster.
3. **ROI over hype** — We sell hours saved and dollars earned, not "AI transformation."
4. **Systems over talent** — Build processes that juniors can execute, not hero workflows.
5. **Margin over volume** — Fewer, higher-value engagements beat a sweatshop.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### WHO they are:
- **Business size:** 10–200 employees
- **Revenue:** $1M–$50M/year
- **Role of buyer:** Owner, CEO, VP of Operations, or Office Manager
- **Age range:** 40–65 (decision-makers who didn't grow up digital)
- **Industry:** Service-based or operationally heavy businesses

### WHERE they are:
- Local/regional businesses (not Silicon Valley startups)
- Active in chambers of commerce, BNI groups, industry associations
- Found on LinkedIn, at trade shows, through referrals

### WHAT they feel:
- Overwhelmed by AI noise but know they're falling behind
- Frustrated by manual, repetitive work their team does daily
- Skeptical of tech vendors who overpromise
- Want proof before commitment

### WHAT they need (but may not say):
- Someone to translate AI into plain English
- A clear, low-risk starting point
- Proof it works before going bigger
- A partner, not a vendor

### Disqualifiers (who we DON'T serve):
- Businesses under $500K revenue (can't afford meaningful engagement)
- Companies that already have a tech team and want custom dev
- Anyone who says "just build me a ChatGPT"
- Tire-kickers with no budget or authority

## Three Target Verticals

### Vertical 1: Professional Services (Law Firms, Accounting, Consulting)

**Why this vertical:**
- High labor costs, heavily document-driven
- Billable hour model means time savings = direct profit
- Partners are decision-makers with budget authority
- Low tech adoption = massive low-hanging fruit

**Pain points:**
- Drowning in email, document review, and admin
- Junior staff spending hours on tasks AI could do in minutes
- No standardized processes across the firm
- Client communication is manual and inconsistent
- Knowledge trapped in senior partners' heads

### Vertical 2: Healthcare & Wellness (Clinics, Dental, Med Spas, Therapy Practices)

**Why this vertical:**
- High patient volume, repetitive admin workflows
- Staff burnout from scheduling, follow-ups, and paperwork
- Patient experience is a competitive differentiator
- Owners are often practitioners, not operators — they need systems

**Pain points:**
- No-shows and last-minute cancellations
- Manual intake forms, follow-ups, and reminders
- Staff overwhelmed by phone calls and appointment juggling
- Inconsistent patient communication
- Difficulty collecting reviews and referrals

### Vertical 3: Home Services & Trades (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Roofing, Landscaping)

**Why this vertical:**
- Owner-operated, lean teams, no tech staff
- Massive volume of inbound leads they lose daily
- Scheduling and dispatch are manual nightmares
- Highest willingness to pay for "done-for-you" solutions
- Very referral-driven (network effects)

**Pain points:**
- Missing calls and losing leads to competitors
- No follow-up system for estimates/quotes
- Scheduling conflicts and inefficient routing
- Inconsistent customer communication
- Zero online reputation management
